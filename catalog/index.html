How to publish your catalog
  1. Pick a folder — every file is hashed into an immutable IPFS CID, right here in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.
  2. Fill in titles, descriptions and tags. Your metadata stays in the exported file, the content stays wherever it already is.
  3. Export the catalog JSON and pin your files + the catalog to IPFS (or keep seeding them from your own node).
  4. Share the catalog CID — anyone can open it in index.lmn and pull the files straight to their drive. Nothing to host, nothing to take down.
